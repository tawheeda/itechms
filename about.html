InfoTech Managed Solutions (ITMS) provides comprehensive IT solutions for small, medium and large enterprises with a reputation for robust, practical delivery.

We have the skills, expertise and capacity to deliver on projects of any size or complexity, driven by safety, excellence and efficiency. Established in 2014 in Emalahleni (Mpumalanga), our footprint quickly expanded beyond the province with a growing global reach.

Vision

To become an internationally recognised and acclaimed analytical IT company.

Mission

  • Provide the ultimate customer experience through excellent service and affordable pricing.
